About Us

Specialist orthodontists across Scotland

Our team of dentists and orthodontists in Scotland strive to provide the best for your smile.

Dental Scotland is one of the largest and fastest growing dental chains in Scotland with 6 practices and growing. We have locations in Glasgow, Falkirk and Stirling. We see over 30,000 patients per year and have officially been in the dentistry industry for over 16 years.

Our Mission

To transform, inspire and empower the lives of the Scottish community, by providing them with optimum dental care at any location across Scotland.

Our Vision

To become the largest dental chain in Scotland, with a practice in every key location. Offering a vast variety of treatment options at affordable prices.
