What is Dental Implants?

Implants are generally considered the best solution when it comes to replacing lost teeth.

Dental implants are a durable and natural-looking solution for missing or damaged teeth. They involve placing a small, biocompatible post into the jawbone, which serves as a replacement tooth root. A custom-made dental crown is then attached to the post, providing a strong and natural-looking replacement tooth. At Dental Scotland, we offer a variety of types of dental implants to meet the unique needs of our patients.

Why use Dental Implants?

Dental implants offer a number of benefits over other tooth replacement options:

  • Improved oral health

  • Comfortable and natural looking

  • Long-lasting

  • Prevent bone loss

  • keep adjacent teeth stable

  • Keep you free of gum disease

What else should I know about Dental Implants

Dental implants are extremely versatile and can be used in the following cases:

  • To retain or replace dentures

  • To support crowns and bridgework

  • To replace unstable or infected crowned teeth

  • To replace a single or multiple missing teeth

Not only can dental implants restore your ability to chew and speak to 100%, they’re the only type of dental restoration that can secure the health of your jawbone. As they’re surgically inserted into the jawbone, the body actively accepts them as if they were a real tooth root, promoting the growth of tissues and cells.

This regeneration means your jawbone stays healthy and free from the risk of resorption, the process whereby the jawbone shrinks away, causing your face to appear sunken. Feel fantastic about chewing, smiling and speaking again with this life-changing treatment.
